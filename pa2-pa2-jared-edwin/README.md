## Move
- 1: W move up 
- 2: S move down 
- 3: D move right 
- 4: A move left 

## Keys
- 1: ESC -Pause Statte only during battle
- 2: h - Reset the health of the player
- 3: r - Respawn all the defeated enemies
- 4: e - Interact with a friend
- 5: b - Spawn the boss in the current area
